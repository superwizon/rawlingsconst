Working Together.To Make Your Project Go Smoothly.

Although it's ideal to have all your finish decisions made prior to the start of a project, we are always willing to make necessary changes to insure your complete satisfaction with the final product. The working relationship between Contractor, Client and Subcontractor plays a huge role in how the project flows and finishes. Communication is our Key to a quality project.

We generally use our own quality subcontractors but welcome new relationships if there is a subcontractor you feel strongly about including.

Protecting Your Investment.

During the job, we take steps to protect the finish and color of your new and old fixtures and appliances. At the end of the job we will discuss maintenance and a warranty walk-thru schedule.
