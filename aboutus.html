Remodeling and Custom Homes.

At Hometown Builders, our focus is on fine custom homes, remodels and additions.

We use only high quality materials. We will discuss money saving options but will not cut corners that may reduce the quality or durability of your home.

As your contractor, we consider ourselves part of your building team. Meetings often include home owners, architects, designers and subcontractors

Subcontractors.

We choose subcontractors that are reliable and friendly. We always encourage positive and negative feedback on our employees and subs.

We generally use our own quality subcontractors but welcome new relationships if there is a subcontractor you feel strongly about including.

We Want You Included In The Process.

Our non-smoking job sites are "visit friendly". We encourage our clients to visit their site any time. During painting, floor finish and other potentially dangerous times, areas of a job may be restricted.

These visits often help make decisions and clarify and questions.
